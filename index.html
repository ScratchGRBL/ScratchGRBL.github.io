SCRATCHGRBL

Hello! I am open source ScratchGRBL. I work as an extension for scratch3.0 to control grbl. 
Why I developed this GRBL1.1 extension for Scratch3.0. One day my son and I were playing with scratch3.0, my son asked me wheather he could play my grbl-Mega-5X mahine with scratch3.0? I thougth it must be ok and tried a lot to search web. But I didn't find any available solution. Then I decided to write my own one. For me it was a little difficult to figure out all code without examples. The scratch hardware extensions like Wedo2 are all connected with bluetooth. But my grbl was connected by an USB cable and communicated with my computer with com port. After searching I found a Scratch3.0 based software called KittenBlock have some examples illustrating how to use USB com port to control hardware. So I decided to write my grbl extension for Scratch3.0 with KittenBlock first and try adopt to Scratch3.0 later on. I called it ScratchGRBL and hope you all like it.
